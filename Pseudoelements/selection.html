Kerala - God's Own Country

Kerala, located in the southwestern region of India, is renowned for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, rich culture, and high literacy rate. Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. Tourists are drawn to its houseboat cruises, Ayurvedic treatments, hill stations, and diverse wildlife. The state is also famous for its traditional art forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, and festivals such as Onam and Vishu.
